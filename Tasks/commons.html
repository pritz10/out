Style Commons

The task explains how to format Commons attributes of a PDF format.
  1. Open a DITAxPresso project.
  2. Click Styler > PDF > Commons.
  3. Click CHAPTER. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  4. Click TITLES. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  5. Click Save.
