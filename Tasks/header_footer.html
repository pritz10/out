Style Page Header and Footer

This task explains how to format the headers and footers for customized output.
  1. Open a DITAxPresso project.
  2. Click Styler > PDF > Header/Footer.
  3. Click HEADER.
    1. Click ODD. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
    2. Click EVEN. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  4. Click FOOTER.
    1. Click ODD. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
    2. Click EVEN. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  5. Click PAGE NUMBER. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  6. Click Save.
